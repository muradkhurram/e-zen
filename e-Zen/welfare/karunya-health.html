What is this?

The Karunya Health framework runs via the centralized Karunya Arogya Suraksha Padhathi (KASP) alongside the integrated Karunya Benevolent Fund (KBF). It provides extensive cashless, paperless medical care and hospitalization benefits covering critical, secondary, and tertiary clinical alignments across registered government and private empanelled hospitals.

Eligibility Parameters

  • The applicant family must be a valid resident of Kerala.
  • Direct onboarding covers all families listed within the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) data or possessing active BPL ration indices.
  • Income Threshold Extension (KBF): Families falling outside standard BPL rows but retaining verified annual revenue indices under ₹3 Lakhs qualify for serious acute ailments (Cancer, Kidney failure, Heart conditions).

Required Verification Folder (Checklist)

💡 Tap markers to monitor verification paperwork requirements at the hospital desk:

  • Ration Card Copy (Priority/Non-Priority) Primary link document used by the hospital kiosk to confirm profile lines.
  • Aadhaar Card Core Identifiers Biometric and structural identification verification source for patient registry records.
  • Revenue-Issued Income Certificate Required specifically to activate KBF critical tier limits if the family holds APL cards.
  • Clinical Diagnostic Logs & Treatment Plan Authorized medical hospital notes certifying the explicitly mapped procedure package.

Procurement & Cashless Process Flow

System Registry: Verified Direct Workflow 2026 Pre-Existing Status: Fully covered from Day 1
Phase 1: Empanelled Hospital Desk Contact
  1. Present the patient’s Ration Card and Aadhaar at the dedicated **KASP/Karunya Counter** inside any empanelled network healthcare facility.
  2. The resident Arogyamitra run an electronic query against the database using your unique identifier.
Phase 2: Pre-Authorization & Matching
  1. The hospital billing desk maps the clinical requirements onto one of the 1,573+ authorized treatment package slots.
  2. An automatic electronic pre-authorization token request routes straight to the State Health Agency dashboard to lock up the funds.
Phase 3: Cashless Treatment & Discharge
  1. Treatment proceeds entirely free of upfront cash demands. The allocation encompasses procedural costs, drugs, diagnostic tests, and up to 15 days of post-discharge monitoring.
  2. The hospital claims processing desk reconciles the finalized treatment notes directly with the SHA electronic ledger portal.

⚠️ Common Institutional Issues & Rejections

  • Non-Empanelled Treatment Attempts: Seeking elective treatment in private institutions that have not entered direct service level agreements with the SHA platform.
  • Mismatched Family Ledger Data: Patient names completely missing from the electronic ration card or national health databases, causing token creation rejections.
  • Package Exhaustion Limits: Bypassing standard base allowance parameters without initiating emergency buffer enhancement steps through medical committee pathways.
