What is this?

The Fishermen Welfare module unifies critical central frameworks like the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) with regional State Fishermen Welfare Fund Boards (Matsyaboard). It delivers essential livelihood security, safety-at-sea monitoring tools, subsidized boat mechanization components, and liquid savings relief during seasonal trawling ban intervals.

Primary Benefits & Allowances

  • Livelihood & Nutritional Support: Direct funding match distributed during active fishing ban/lean months. Registered beneficiaries contribute a small monthly sum, matched by central and state shares, to provide a lump sum safety cushion.
  • Fleet Mechanization Subsidies: 40% to 60% direct cost subventions for procurement of outboard motors, specialized deep-sea fishing nets, and safety tracking transponders.
  • Group Accident Insurance Scheme: Complete premium-free risk coverage providing financial compensation up to ₹5 Lakhs for death or permanent disability incurred during active sea routing operations.

  • Traditional Fisherman Certificate Issued by your village officer or local fisheries unit inspector confirming reliance on allied traditional methods.

Onboarding & Disbursement Steps

System Timeline: 2026 Operational Directives Audit Path: Verification records managed by District Fisheries Desk
Phase 1: Fishermen Board Registry Check
  1. Log into your State Fisheries management portal or visit your regional Fisheries Village Extension Office.
  2. Verify that your unique biometric identification row or Matsyaboard contribution account remains active and continuous.
Phase 2: Lean Relief / Subsidy Claim Logging
  1. Select the targeted welfare service branch (e.g., Lean Season Savings Relief or Navigation Equipment Grant).
  2. Upload highly legible scans of your biometric ID sheet, vessel license registry numbers, and active bank details.
  3. Secure your file submission to generate an application acknowledgment string.
Phase 3: Field Inspector Review & Payout
  1. The Local Fisheries Inspector checks files against physical coastal ward logs to prevent dual-claim setups.
  2. Approved allocations drop directly through the centralized direct benefit network lines into your verified bank account.

⚠️ Common Procedural Blocks & Failures

  • Contribution Discontinuity: Forfeiting or missing the required matching monthly savings contributions breaks the validation string for lean season relief.
  • Unlicensed Boat Deployments: Submitting equipment subsidy claims linked to vessels that lack a valid, active license on the ReALCraft registry.
  • Mismatched Identity Metadata: Slight spelling or naming variations across your traditional fisherman status certificate, biometric ID card, and core Aadhaar fields.
