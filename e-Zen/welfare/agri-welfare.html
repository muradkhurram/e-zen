What is this?

The Agricultural Welfare framework integrates central liquid income interventions alongside localized state field mechanisms managed directly by your neighborhood Krishi Bhavan. The structure combines baseline financial income stability, subsidized biological inputs, advanced farm mechanization support, and dedicated agrarian pension funds.

Core Operational Verticals

  • PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi: Direct centralized income assistance providing ₹6,000 annually, divided into three balanced electronic tranches.
  • Kerala Karshaka Kshemagam: Specialized welfare fund mechanism extending a monthly financial pension of ₹1,000 to registered old-age farmers (60+).
  • Krishi Bhavan Input & Irrigation Subsidies: Allocation models offering 50–85% direct price discounts on quality seeds, bio-fertilizers, and localized modern drip/sprinkler deployments under the integrated **Per Drop More Crop (PDMC)** system.

Onboarding & Subsidy Request Pipeline

System Layout: Active 2026 Registry Framework Digital Integration: Automated land record cross-matching enabled
Phase 1: Digital Registration & e-KYC
  1. Access the central PM-Kisan hub or the state AIMS agriculture portal.
  2. Log in using your Aadhaar credentials and execute the required face/biometric e-KYC sequence to secure active eligibility marks.
Phase 2: Krishi Bhavan Mapping
  1. Submit your land tax metadata identifiers to your village-level Krishi Bhavan office desk.
  2. The Agricultural Officer reviews land boundaries and flags the profile for direct seasonal seed distributions or micro-irrigation component subsidies.
Phase 3: Direct Benefit Clearance
  1. Once verified, seasonal input subsidy components can be claimed straight from authorized local storage depots.
  2. Cash tranches route via the automated central clearing house directly into your Aadhaar-seeded financial ledger.

⚠️ Common Procedural Blocks & Failures

  • Un-updated Land Revenue Records: Attempting to register using historic inherited land deeds that have not been mutated cleanly in the modern computerized revenue system ledger.
  • Aadhaar-Bank Seeding Invalidation: Banking profiles missing explicit direct benefit links, resulting in payment engine errors during tranche distribution sweeps.
  • Institutional Exclusion Flags: Applicants holding institutional land ownership or individuals drawing regular government pensions outside explicit agrarian welfare limits.
