What is this?

The Labour Welfare Benefits module serves as a primary social protection network for non-elite, unorganized sector workers (such as construction operators, daily casual labourers, and headload handlers). Registration hooks directly into your state’s Labor Welfare Board or the centralized e-Shram registry, granting formal access to essential lifestyle allowances, safety equipment components, and family health stipends.

Core Benefits Provided

  • Maternity & Marriage Benefits: Financial cash grants allocated to registered women workers or toward the marriage layout expenses of a worker's dependent children.
  • Educational Scholarships: Tiered annual cash stipends provided to the children of registered laborers to ensure continuity in secondary and technical professional colleges.
  • Chronic Illness & Funeral Support: Medical reimbursement models for high-intensity treatments alongside instant cash disbursals for immediate family funeral costs.

Required Verification Folder (Checklist)

💡 Tap targets to confirm specific paperwork configuration arrangements:

  • e-Shram Card / Board Registration Passbook Must display a valid 12-digit Universal Account Number (UAN) string confirming active registration.
  • 90-Day Employment Certification Slip Signed declaration from a registered contractor, trade union executive, or labor officer verifying at least 90 days of active work in the past year.
  • Aadhaar Profile & Bank Passbook Leaf Copy Core mapping identifiers required to process electronic Direct Benefit Transfer routing without errors.
  • Dependent Relationship Proof (If Claiming Education/Marriage Grants) Legitimate School Transfer Certificate, Ration Card link rows, or Birth Certificates.

Filing & Claims Pipeline

System Status: 2026 Core Registration Module Mandatory Term: Annual membership renewal preserves active status
Phase 1: Verification of UAN Status
  1. Access your state's online Labour Welfare Board portal or log into the central e-Shram terminal listed above.
  2. Verify your profile parameters and confirm your annual subscription fees are fully settled to keep the account active.
Phase 2: Specific Benefit Application
  1. Select **Apply for Board Welfare Scheme** and choose the explicit option required (e.g., Education Allowance or Maternity Grant).
  2. Upload clean scans of your 90-day work certificate alongside the targeted beneficiary proof files (such as child’s marksheets or bills).
  3. Submit your file layout to secure a digital reference tracking receipt.
Phase 3: Labour Officer Audit & Clearance
  1. The District Assistant Labour Officer reviews files and runs a background query against unorganized sector employment ledgers.
  2. Upon institutional approval, the financial grant drops directly via the DBT clearinghouse into your registered bank account.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Lapsed Annual Renewals: Allowing your Labor Board registration membership to pass past its due date renders all welfare claims inactive.
  • Invalid 90-Day Employment Certificates: Submitting work history sheets that lack valid contractor license numbers or official trade union stamps.
  • Dual Benefit Overlaps: Attempting to claim educational or medical payouts for a child while already drawing an identical grant from an institutional scholarship source.
