What is this?

This digital tracking service enables Ration Card holders to check their authorized monthly food grain quotas and retail pricing structures in real-time. It provides complete, transparent access to what your family is legally entitled to lift from your local ration shop before you make a physical purchase.

Why is it needed?

Monthly statutory commodity parameters fluctuate based on central state pool allocations, seasonal updates, and household metrics (Antyodaya Anna Yojana, Priority, or Non-Priority card brackets). Checking entitlements online ensures retail transparency, prevents unauthorized dealers from under-allocating stocks, and lists precise current costs.

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: Civil Supplies Dept. Circular
Before You Begin
  • Know Your Card Color: Allocation calculations dynamically adjust to Yellow, Pink, Blue, or White card categories.
  • Understand Validity: Entitlement databases reset on the 1st day of every calendar month.
Phase 1: Query Input
  1. Navigate to the Citizen Services section on the official Civil Supplies e-portal.
  2. Click the dedicated link marked "Check Monthly PDS Entitlements / Stock Allocation".
  3. Input your valid 10-digit Ration Card registration string into the field box.
Phase 2: Live Verification
  1. Enter the captcha authorization puzzle to pass automated script verifications.
  2. Select the target allocation Month and Year parameters from the context dropdown listings.
  3. Click on the 'Submit / Fetch Data' action prompt to run a secure query check.
Phase 3: Balance Sheet Generation
  1. The system generates a breakdown showing your item allocations (Rice, Wheat, Kerosene, Sugar, etc.).
  2. Review details including the total eligible weight units, price per kilogram, and already lifted balances.
  3. Optionally save or snapshot the digital allocation balance sheet receipt to reference at your dealer.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Invalid Card String: Typing mistakes or formatting errors when entering your 10-digit registration number.
  • Aadhaar De-linking: Blocked status alerts occurring if the household profile has been flagged for missing biometric seeds.
  • Database Offline Status: Periodic synchronization maintenance down periods matching local retail e-POS upload schedules.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Allocations displaying as zero values? If your current monthly allotment reads zero, confirm your household category hasn't changed or check if another member already lifted the full family quota.


Mismatched shop location data showing? If the displayed ARD number doesn't match where you live, check your transfer history to confirm a previous migration request wasn't cancelled or delayed.

FAQ

Q: Can I access my Kerala PDS allocation data from other states?
A: Yes. Under the integrated 'One Nation One Ration Card' (ONORC) plan, active entitlements can be queried and claimed out-of-state using your linked biometric numbers.


Q: Does an unused monthly grain entitlement roll over to next month?
A: No. Unclaimed statutory balances lapse at the end of each calendar month and cannot be carried forward into future distribution cycles.
