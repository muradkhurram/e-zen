What is this?

This recurrent public utility collection framework tracks and processes annual property tax (building/holding tax) assessed on built structures located inside Grama Panchayats, Municipalities, and Corporations across Kerala.

Why is it needed?

Paying your annual property tax keeps your local structural ownership files in good legal standing. Settlement receipts are required for securing utility connections, updating land ownership titles, and obtaining building numbers or commercial licenses from local authorities.

Required Details (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered:

  • Building Number & Ward Number The alpha-numeric codes mapped to your door layout (e.g., 12/432-A) found on old receipts
  • Local Body Identification The specific District and Grama Panchayat/Municipality/Corporation your property falls under
  • Digital Payment Tool UPI Apps, NetBanking credentials, or active Credit/Debit cards

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: LSGD Revenue Operations Cell
Before You Begin
  • Quick Pay vs Login: Use the "Citizen Login" path for deep property transaction logs or historical analysis, or select "Quick Pay" for fast checkout.
  • Verify Format: Note the difference between older physical register sequences and newer digital building numbers before typing.
Phase 1: Geographic Filtration
  1. Access the official Sanchaya payment portal platform link.
  2. Select your property's administrative group type: "Corporation", "Municipality", or "Grama Panchayat".
  3. Choose your District from the first dropdown menu, which populates the second list with matching regional local body names. Click "Select".
Phase 2: Locating Property Ledger Dues
  1. Click "Quick Pay" on the service sub-menu pane to bypass registration forms.
  2. Enter your designated Ward Year, your active Ward Number, and your primary Building Number sequence fields. Click "Search".
  3. Review the displayed property profile block matching your name, location details, and computed seasonal dues tracking line items.
Phase 3: Checkout Allocation
  1. Check the tick-boxes for the specific target assessment periods you want to settle (current year, half-years, or past arrears).
  2. Provide your contact phone number and email address to route automated transaction tracking receipts.
  3. Choose your gateway option (e.g., SBI Multi-Option Payment, BillDesk), authorize checkout credentials, and print the resulting official tax receipt.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Mismatched Alpha Character Splits: Typing building extensions (like sub-room tags /A, /B) using incorrect dash or slash formats.
  • Unmapped Ward Structural Splits: Failing to adjust your lookup criteria after your local area undergoes recent ward boundary reshaping.
  • Double Payment Double Locks: Payment connections locking up if you refresh the browser tab during a slow banking settlement process.

🔧 Troubleshooting

System displays "No Records Found" for your correct door number? This can happen if the local body database format varies. Try using the alternative "Search by Owner Name" tracking index tool on the same portal layout to find the exact database record string.


Funds debited from bank but Sanchaya status shows unpaid? Do not make a second payment. The system's internal accounting registers normally resolve pending bank statuses within 3 working days.

FAQ

Q: Are property taxes calculated flat across all structures in Kerala?
A: No. Sanchaya dynamically computes tax dues based on plinth area square footage, construction categories (concrete vs tile roofing), usage types (residential vs commercial), and proximity to prime thoroughfares.


Q: Can I check past years' tax receipts on the quick pay portal?
A: The Quick Pay flow only shows pending bills. To review your complete payment history, create a verified account using the "Citizen Login" interface.
