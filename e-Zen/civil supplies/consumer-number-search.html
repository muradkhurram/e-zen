Consumer Number Search

Issuing Authority Kerala State Electricity Board (KSEB)
Official Portal KSEB Web Self Service Portal
Prescribed Fee Free of Cost
Processing Time Instant (Real-Time)
Go to Official Portal ↗

What is this?

This digital utility lookup tracking tool allows power consumers to recover their unique 13-digit KSEB Consumer Number if they do not have a physical bill. It securely cross-references registration profiles against state grid databases via linked communication tags.

Why is it needed?

The 13-digit Consumer Number is the primary identity code required to check energy balances, run online bill payments, request load changes, or register structural electrical faults. Finding this code electronically eliminates the need to visit local electrical section offices when physical printouts are missing.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered:

  • Registered Mobile Number The phone number registered with your electrical section layout profile
  • Aadhaar Card or PAN Card Required to verify consumer ownership identity if executing a fallback look-up

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: KSEB Information Systems IT Wing
Before You Begin
  • Know Your Section: Keep your local Electrical Section name ready (e.g., Kazhakkoottam, Vyttila) to narrow parameters.
  • Network Connectivity: Ensure your registered SIM card can receive inbound SMS verification tokens.
Phase 1: Portal Navigation
  1. Open the official KSEB Web Self Service portal link.
  2. Navigate to the left panel grid choices and select "Quick Pay / Know Your Consumer Number".
  3. Click on the fallback tool option marked "Search Consumer Number by Mobile / Name".
Phase 2: Metric Filtering & Authentication
  1. Select your matching Electrical Circle, Division, and local Section Office from the dropdown menus.
  2. Input your registered 10-digit mobile number or the exact spelling string of the consumer's name.
  3. Trigger the validation prompt and enter the security token SMS code sent to your handset.
Phase 3: Record Extraction
  1. The matching database rows display on your screen area layout.
  2. Verify the displayed premises door numbers or name text blocks to ensure it is your property connection.
  3. Note down the 13-digit Consumer Number shown next to your consumer details block.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Unlinked Mobile Numbers: Search errors occurring if your phone number is not seeded into the KSEB database profile.
  • Section Office Selection Error: Choosing the wrong local section branch, which blocks the query from locating your file records.
  • Name Formatting Discrepancies: Spelling layout inputs that differ from the text printed on your original connection registry.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Mobile search yielding zero matching metrics? If searching by phone number fails, try using an old paper bill receipt to find your section name, or use the alternative 'Search by Name and Consumer Linkage' option.


SMS authorization tokens failing to arrive? Check if your phone provider is blocking automated messages or delay the request by 5 minutes to avoid server request spikes.

FAQ

Q: Does my KSEB 13-digit Consumer Number change if I change my meter device?
A: No. The consumer number is permanently tied to the structural property location. It remains unchanged during physical equipment replacements.


Q: Can I update my unlinked phone number online?
A: Yes. You can register your new number by navigating to the 'Update Contact Profile Details' link on the KSEB web self-service dashboard.
