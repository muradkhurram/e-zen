What is this?

This service allows property owners, tenants, or commercial entities to apply for a fresh, legal electricity supply connection directly from the state power grid. The platform manages the entire workflow from initial documentation to site verification and final digital payment setup.

Why is it needed?

Whether commissioning a newly built domestic house, opening a retail commercial store, or setting up a temporary construction site, getting standard metered power requires administrative clearance. Registering online sets up transparent processing, tracks queue placement, and ensures compliance with Kerala State Electricity Regulatory Commission codes.

  • Identity Proof Aadhaar Card, Voters ID, Passport, or PAN Card of the applicant

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: KSEB Supply Code Guidelines
Before You Begin
  • Determine Load Requirements: Compute your total internal appliance wattage to decide whether you need a Single Phase or Three Phase setup.
  • Wiring Scheme: Ensure a certified electrical contractor has successfully completed the internal wiring and issued a test certificate.
Phase 1: Profile Creation & Application Input
  1. Access the main KSEB Web Self Service interface page.
  2. Click "New Connection Application" and create a portal identity linked to your mobile phone.
  3. Select the target geographical Section Office and choose your Tariff Code (e.g., LT-1A for domestic residences).
Phase 2: Technical Mapping & Uploads
  1. Input specific data including connected load in Watts, building numbers, and adjacent consumer numbers for grid referencing.
  2. Upload digital scans of ownership documents, identification certificates, and internal layout maps in PDF form.
  3. Submit the layout draft and pay the minimal initial document registration fee via the secure payment portal.
Phase 3: Field Inspection & Meter Installation
  1. An assistant engineer from your local section conducts a site feasibility check to evaluate line or post distances.
  2. Pay the formal security deposit and service connection charges generated after inspection approval.
  3. The service line is drawn and the physical metered connection is activated by the technical crew.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Arrears on Property Plot: Connection blocks occurring if the target land plot has unpaid bills left over from a previous tenant or building structure.
  • Encroachment Zone Conflicts: Rejections if the construction falls under disputed property lines or stays too close to existing high-voltage grid corridors.
  • Incomplete Wiring Certifications: Failure to provide a valid safety certification test report signed by a licensed electrical wireman.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Distance to nearest distribution post exceeds 30 meters? If the line draw exceeds normal lengths, the system automatically adds standard post extension or structure charges to your estimate breakdown sheet.


Application status stuck on "Feasibility Pending"? This typically means the regional inspection team faces local logistics backlog. Use your portal application token ID to trigger an update ping to the sub-engineer.

FAQ

Q: Can I apply for a power meter connection without a building number?
A: Yes. You can get a temporary or special provisional card code line connection by submitting an authorized ownership declaration alongside local body land documents.


Q: What is the normal timeline for meter installations after settling final charges?
A: Once all fees and compliance parameters clear, crews normally mount and wire physical meters within five working days under standard grid conditions.
