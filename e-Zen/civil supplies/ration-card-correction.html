Ration Card Correction

Issuing Authority Taluk Supply Office (TSO)
Official Portal Civil Supplies e-Portal
Prescribed Fee Nil (Service charges apply)
Processing Time 7–15 Days
Go to Official Portal ↗

What is this?

This service facilitates modifications to inaccurate or outdated particulars recorded on an existing family Ration Card. It allows citizens to correct typing mistakes, update structural records, alter misspelled names, rectify birth dates, adjust relationships, or amend incorrect job details across family listings.

Why is it needed?

Mismatched demographics between your Ration Card and Aadhaar database can completely block monthly biometric point-of-sale e-POS distribution checks at ration shops. Keeping these records flawlessly aligned prevents administrative delivery dropouts and satisfies crucial compliance parameters for linking state-sponsored health insurance and welfare grants.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered:

  • Aadhaar Card Primary reference document containing corrected target data
  • Supporting Proof for Correction Birth Certificate, SSLC book, or Passport (for name and date of birth corrections)
  • Original Ration Card copy Scanned copy of the front page and internal member data sheets

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: Civil Supplies Dept. Circular
Before You Begin
  • Update Aadhaar First: Ensure your target updates are successfully integrated into your Aadhaar card before modifying your ration profile.
  • Format Specifications: Scan authentic physical government documents; avoid uploading low-resolution smartphone camera pictures.
Phase 1: Application Initialization
  1. Access the official Civil Supplies e-portal interface.
  2. Authenticate your secure citizen session profile using Aadhaar OTP credentials.
  3. Locate and click the "Ration Card Correction / Change Details" module link.
Phase 2: Correcting Records & Verification
  1. Select the specific household member profile requiring typographical or details adjustments.
  2. Overtype data cells (Name, Age, DOB, Gender, or Occupation) to match your updated identification documents.
  3. Upload clean, high-resolution PDF proof certificates backing up the demographic corrections.
Phase 3: Inspection & Final Download
  1. The workflow updates are securely transferred to the respective Taluk Supply Officer (TSO) for database validation.
  2. Inspectors verify submitted digital application parameters against internal state registration records.
  3. Download the newly amended electronic E-Ration Card summary layout directly from your portal dashboard.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Aadhaar Information Incongruence: Inputting corrected values that still conflict with data on the central UIDAI database.
  • Ineligible Support Documentation: Uploading self-attested notes or unofficial letters that lack standard certification seals from local authorities.
  • Illegible Digital Scans: Files that are blurry, cut off, or heavily compressed to meet size requirements, rendering names or dates unreadable.

🔧 Troubleshooting

System error during verification checks? If data corrections do not save, check if your local web browser is blocking automated pop-ups or secure credential verifications. Clearing cache files usually resolves this.


Correction not reflecting at ration shops? Database syncs with retail point-of-sale (e-POS) devices can take up to 48 hours post-TSO approval. Wait 2 days before checking your status at local distribution counters.

FAQ

Q: Can I change the Head of Family (HOF) name through this correction link?
A: Minor spelling corrections can be updated here, but structural HOF transfers (e.g., due to passing) require a separate dedicated application track.


Q: Is there an official penalty fee for making corrections?
A: No administrative penalties apply. Only nominal processing or Akshaya portal transaction service fees apply depending on submission paths.
