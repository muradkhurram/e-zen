What is this?

This dashboard manages liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) cooking fuel subsidy records under the PAHAL (DBTL) infrastructure scheme. It lets consumers check their payment transfer histories, link banking profiles, upload verification identities, and manage their opt-in or opt-out subsidy statuses online.

Why is it needed?

Domestic cylinder refills are purchased at full market rates, after which government subsidy balances reimburse directly into verified bank savings accounts via automated Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). Reviewing your setup regularly ensures your consumer connection links match your Aadhaar seed entries, preventing payment dropouts.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered:

  • 17-Digit LPG Consumer ID Found on your physical gas passbook or monthly refill cash memos
  • Aadhaar Card (Linked to Bank Account) Required to authenticate and route your direct benefit transfers
  • Bank Passbook Copy Clear scan of the page showing your IFSC code and account details

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: Ministry Guidelines Circular
Before You Begin
  • Know Your Agency: Identify your oil marketing company provider (Indane, HP Gas, or Bharat Gas).
  • NPCI Seeding Check: Verify your target bank account is seeded with the NPCI mapping matrix to receive direct benefit payments.
Phase 1: Brand Verification
  1. Access the comprehensive MyLPG central web interface portal.
  2. Select your specific LPG provider company icon link (Indane, HP, or Bharat Gas).
  3. Log in to your dashboard account using your registered credentials or mobile profile.
Phase 2: Status Assessment & Mapping
  1. Click on the user dashboard panel labeled "View Subsidy Transfer History / Track Profile".
  2. Review your Aadhaar-linking verification metrics and active banking route data displays.
  3. If missing links are found, fill out the online profile updating sheet with your bank account parameters.
Phase 3: Update Processing
  1. Upload your scanned identity files and bank summary verification certificates in PDF layout formats.
  2. Submit your update profile forms to route the requests directly to your local distribution office.
  3. Track your verification progress token via the reference number history sheet panel.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • NPCI Mapping Mismatches: Blocked transfers occurring if your Aadhaar number is linked to a different or closed account.
  • Name String Variations: Data conflicts due to name variations between your LPG subscription file and your bank account text.
  • Income Cap Exclusions: Legal restrictions stopping subsidies if a household's annual taxable income exceeds designated statutory ceilings.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Cylinder delivered but no subsidy received? Processing and routing clear updates across bank servers can take up to 3 working days after delivery. Check your digital profile overview first before opening an issue.


Status shows as 'De-activated'? If your connection reads as de-activated, visit your local agency distribution center to complete standard physical verification updates and re-activate your profile status.

FAQ

Q: Where do I look up my exact 17-digit LPG consumer identification string?
A: Your unique 17-digit customer ID is prominently displayed on the front layout sheet of your physical passbook booklet or on your printed invoice sheets.


Q: Can I change my subsidy-receiving bank account online?
A: Yes. You can submit updated bank passbook uploads and IFSC profile modifications through this portal link at any time.
