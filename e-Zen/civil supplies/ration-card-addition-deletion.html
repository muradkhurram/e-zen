Ration Card Member Addition/Deletion

Issuing Authority Taluk Supply Office (TSO)
Official Portal Civil Supplies e-Portal
Prescribed Fee Nil (Service charges apply)
Processing Time 7–15 Days
Go to Official Portal ↗

What is this?

This service allows citizens to modify an existing household Ration Card by either adding a new member (due to birth or marriage) or removing an existing member (due to death, marriage, or relocation). This keeps the family structure officially synchronized with the Public Distribution System (PDS).

Why is it needed?

Accurate household data is essential for managing your statutory commodity quotas, as food grain allocations are distributed per individual unit. It is legally required to avoid biometric duplication errors, remove deceased members, and secure specific Deletion Certificates needed for newly married individuals to register under their new family card.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered:

  • Aadhaar Card Required for the individual being added or deleted (UID mapping)
  • Birth Certificate or Marriage Certificate Mandatory for addition (Birth proof for newborns; Marriage proof for newlyweds)
  • Official Deletion Certificate Mandatory for addition if the individual was previously registered on another family card
  • Death Certificate Mandatory for member deletion requests arising due to demise

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: Civil Supplies Dept. Circular
Before You Begin
  • Verify Original Card: Ensure your existing family Ration Card is active and not currently suspended or locked.
  • Check Aadhaar Links: The individual being processed must have a valid Aadhaar number with up-to-date demographic text.
Phase 1: Portal Authentication
  1. Log in to the official Civil Supplies e-portal using the cardholder credentials.
  2. Authenticate using the registered citizen mobile number and your Aadhaar-linked OTP profile.
  3. Select the respective operational dashboard: "Member Addition" or "Member Deletion".
Phase 2: Information Update & Upload
  1. For Additions: Enter demographic details exactly as in Aadhaar, and input the legal relation to the Head of Family (HOF).
  2. For Deletions: Select the specific family member from the grid and state the reason (Death, Separation, or Marriage).
  3. Upload clean, legible copies of the relevant certificates (Birth, Death, Marriage, or Deletion) in PDF format.
Phase 3: Processing & Card Upgraded
  1. The workflow routes directly to your local Taluk Supply Officer (TSO) for desk evaluation.
  2. Upon approval, the system re-calculates your household's active unit weight and subsidy volumes.
  3. Download the refreshed digital E-Ration Card featuring the modified registry directly from the portal.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Dual Registration Faults: Attempting to add an individual who has not been legally deleted from their prior family Ration Card.
  • Spelling Divergence: Name strings or structural data inputs differing from the verified data on the Aadhaar database.
  • Unsigned Certifications: Uploading informal letters or incomplete hospital slips instead of legally valid certificates issued by local authorities.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Member missing from system dropdowns? If an existing member does not appear for processing, verify if their Aadhaar seed status is marked active under the main "View Card Status" dashboard.


Certificate validation failures? Ensure scanned certificate uploads are under the maximum size ceilings specified by the portal and are completely clear, with all official stamps and signatures visible.

FAQ

Q: Can I add a newborn baby without an Aadhaar Card?
A: For infants below 5 years, some structural exceptions allow initial addition via Birth Certificate, but an updated Aadhaar number must be seeded within the mandated period.


Q: What is a Deletion Certificate and where do I get it?
A: It is an official document proving a member has been removed from a card. It is automatically generated online when a member deletion request is successfully approved by the TSO.
