Ration Card Transfer

Issuing Authority Taluk Supply Office (TSO)
Official Portal Civil Supplies e-Portal
Prescribed Fee Nil (Service charges apply)
Processing Time 7–12 Days
Go to Official Portal ↗

What is this?

This service facilitates the reallocation of a Ration Card or specific card members to a different jurisdiction. This structure is deployed when shifting a card to a new Taluk, shifting to a different authorized retail ration shop (ARD) within the same Taluk, or migrating registration due to long-term physical relocations.

Why is it needed?

Ration commodity allocation and biometric delivery pipelines are directly tethered to a specified neighborhood retail shop (ARD). If you permanently change your residence across borders or local administrative blocks without formally processing an administrative transfer, your family's ability to fetch essential grain allocations locally will remain geoblocked.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered:

  • Current Original Ration Card Clear digital scan of the front cover and complete registry list
  • New Residential Address Proof Land tax receipt, registered lease deed, or village office certificate matching the target address
  • Aadhaar Card copy Required for all migrating members to confirm unified biometric profiles

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: 21 June 2026 Source: Civil Supplies Dept. Circular
Before You Begin
  • Settle Outstanding Quotas: Collect your current month's commodities prior to initiating the transfer to avoid inventory processing locks.
  • Identify New Shop Number: Note the official ARD (Authorized Retail Dealer) shop number code of your target location beforehand.
Phase 1: Verification & Module Selection
  1. Log into your account dashboard via the secure Civil Supplies e-portal framework.
  2. Authenticate identity using your primary Aadhaar-registered mobile number and verification code.
  3. Locate and navigate directly into the "Ration Card Transfer / Shifting" operational panel.
Phase 2: Target Mapping & Document Upload
  1. Specify whether you are executing a "Full Card Transfer" or shifting select members out of the group.
  2. Input targeted geographical fields including District, Taluk, Local Body, and the specific target ARD Shop Code.
  3. Upload valid residential certificate documents and structural identification files in clean PDF format.
Phase 3: TSO Validation & Fresh Copy Issuance
  1. The application transfers across dashboards to the respective Taluk Supply Officer (TSO) for structural evaluation.
  2. Upon confirmation of territorial boundaries, the old ARD link is broken and updated to the new mapping matrix.
  3. Download your freshly generated updated Digital E-Ration Card carrying your new residential allocation detail.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Invalid ARD Shop Number: Providing incorrect or non-existent retail dealer numbers during application mapping.
  • Address Mismatch: Discrepancies between inputted geographical zones and uploaded local residence certificate parameters.
  • Pending Family Splitting Conflicts: Attempting a full card transfer while individual member deletion requests are actively processing.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Target ARD not displaying in list? If your chosen local retail dealer doesn't show up, ensure you selected the correct local Village or ward location first, as dropdown menus filter progressively.


Biometrics failing right after transfer? It takes around 48 hours for updated routing profiles to push onto local point-of-sale devices. Please wait 2 days before attempting your initial local withdrawal.

FAQ

Q: Can I transfer my card to another district within Kerala?
A: Yes. You can process an inter-district transfer smoothly online by choosing the targeted District and corresponding Taluk entry options.


Q: Is there any physical card verification required at the office?
A: No. The process is completely paperless. Once approved online, your updated digital card remains legally valid at your new distribution shop.
