What is this?

An Indian Passport is a globally tracked sovereign travel document issued by the Ministry of External Affairs. It certifies the holder's national identity and citizenship status abroad, serving as a legal prerequisite for international border crossings, visa issuance, and consular protection.

Why is it needed?

A passport is legally mandatory for any form of foreign travel, overseas employment, or university study abroad. Domestically, it represents one of the highest-tier government credentials, widely recognized for strict residential address verification and identity authentication by financial institutions and security authorities.

Eligibility

  • Citizenship: Applicants must be bona fide citizens of India.
  • Age Profiles: Open to all age groups including infants (minors under 15 are issued 5-year passports, while adults receive standard 10-year validity books).
  • ECR vs Non-ECR Category: Applicants who have passed matriculation (10th standard) or possess higher educational degrees are eligible for the "Non-ECR" status, exempting them from mandatory emigration checks when traveling for work to specified countries.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what original items you need to carry to your appointment:

  • Proof of Present Address (Choose One) Aadhaar Card, Active Bank Passbook with a photograph, Water/Electricity bill, or a Registered Rent Agreement.
  • Proof of Date of Birth (Choose One) Birth Certificate issued by a municipal authority, Matriculation/School Leaving Certificate, PAN Card, or Driving Licence.
  • Non-ECR Category Proof (Optional) Original 10th Standard passing certificate or higher degree qualification sheets to waive emigration check tracking.

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: June 2026 Source: Ministry of External Affairs Portal Guidelines
Before You Begin
  • 📱 Check Aadhaar Alignment: Ensure that your name spelling and birth date are exactly identical across both your Aadhaar card and your educational records. A mismatch will cause data freezes during the face-to-face review stage.
Phase 1: Online Form Submission & Payment
  1. Click Register on Passport Seva Portal above and complete the initial account registration. Select your nearest regional passport office boundary.
  2. Log in with your credentials and select Apply for Fresh Passport/Re-issue of Passport.
  3. Choose the Online Application Form option to type directly into the browser sheet.
  4. Select application parameters: **Fresh Passport**, **Normal Mode** (or Tatkaal), and **36 Pages** booklet choice.
  5. Fill in your demographic data, structural family names, and present residence timeline parameters exactly as printed on your proof papers.
  6. Review the data structure, hit submit, and proceed to the **Pay and Schedule Appointment** panel. Pay the statutory ₹1,500 online fee to open calendar slot picking. Select your preferred Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) venue and secure a booking slot.
Phase 2: Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) Verification Day
  1. Arrive at your designated PSK venue 15 minutes before your time slot. Carry your printed application slip and all original checklist documents along with self-attested photocopies.
  2. Counter A (Biometrics): A specialist operator will capture your digital facial profile photo, scan your fingerprints, and verify your online form fields.
  3. Counter B (Verification): A verification officer inspects your original documents line-by-line to verify alignment with government archives.
  4. Counter C (Granting): A senior official grants final approval based on the review metrics and sets off the automated police routing workflow. You will receive an exit slip with a file reference token sequence.
Phase 3: Police Verification & Post Delivery
  1. Within 3-7 days after your PSK session, your local police station tracking engine will assign an officer to verify your residency profile.
  2. The verification officer will contact you to inspect your physical premises or require you to visit the station house carrying your neighborhood proofs and two local witness statements.
  3. Once the police desk submits a "Clear" status log back to the regional portal, the passport booklet goes into centralized high-security printing queues.
  4. The finalized booklet is packaged and dispatched directly to your residential address via India Post Speed Post.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Discrepancies in Birth Records: Carrying educational mark sheets where the date of birth or name characters vary from your local municipal birth certificate lines.
  • Present Address Tenure Shortfalls: If you have lived at your current address for less than one continuous year and omit your previous residential data matrix, the security scan engine will flag it for data concealment.
  • Unclear Address Proof Structures: Submitting non-authorized local private bank passbooks or unofficial unnotarized lease layouts that do not meet regulatory standards.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Did your appointment booking fail or did the portal time out during checkout processing?
Do not trigger a fresh payment transaction immediately. Portal policies state that payments can take up to 24-48 hours to reconcile. Go back to your application dashboard page, select the target file block, click **Track Payment Status**, and select the refresh link to restore validation without lose of capital.

FAQ

Q: What happens if I miss my scheduled appointment slot at the Passport Seva Kendra?
A: A single online payment allows up to **three appointment reschedules** within a 12-month window from the initial transaction date. If you exhaust all three options, the payment expires and you must pay again.


Q: Is it mandatory for minors to have their parents' names updated on their own passports?
A: Yes. For minor applications, it is highly recommended to carry at least one parent's original passport with matching name entries to fast-track verification loops at Counter B.
