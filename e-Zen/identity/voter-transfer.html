What is this?

Voter ID Transfer is the structural administrative procedure to move your registered electoral voting entry from your old residential location to your new home address. Handled digitally using **Form 8**, this workflow updates your voting location to your new polling station booth while seamlessly maintaining your original 10-character EPIC card identity profile sequence.

Why is it needed?

By constitutional law, you can only hold a voter listing at your current actual place of residence. Transferring your voter profile is required when shifting houses permanently—whether moving to another street down the block or moving across states—ensuring you are legally registered to vote in your new neighborhood's local and state elections.

Eligibility

  • Existing Registered Voters: You must already possess a valid Voter ID (EPIC) card profile registered somewhere within India.
  • Change of Residence: You must have physically relocated your home address base, either within the same assembly constituency boundary or to an entirely different constituency.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what you have gathered to scan and upload:

  • Existing Card Metrics Your 10-character alphanumeric EPIC card number (found directly on the face of your current card).
  • Proof of New Residence (Choose One) Water, Electricity, or Gas Connection Bill, Passport, Bank Passbook, or a registered Rent/Lease Agreement matching the new address.
  • Photograph (If needed) A crisp passport-size color photograph against a plain white background (JPEG format under 2MB) if your current card photo is outdated.

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: June 2026 Source: Election Commission of India Electoral Operations Desk
Before You Begin
  • 🔒 Do Not Apply for a New Card: Never fill out a fresh Form 6 for a transfer. This creates illegal double-registration flags. Always submit Form 8 to safely migrate your existing record.
Phase 1: Portal Access & Objective Selection
  1. Click Open Voters' Service Portal above to open the official ECI portal.
  2. Log in using your verified mobile number and password, or quickly complete the OTP registration step if visiting for the first time.
  3. Click on the card options labeled Shifted to other place / Correction of entries in existing electoral roll (**Form 8**).
  4. Select **Self**, enter your alphanumeric EPIC card number, and submit to verify your current live profile record.
Phase 2: Filling Out Form 8 Shifting Modules
  1. A popup selection window will display. Choose the application reason: **Shifting of Residence**.
  2. Select your relocation classification: **Within Assembly Constituency** (if moving nearby) or **Outside Assembly Constituency** (if shifting to a different district or state boundary).
  3. Type in your new residential parameters: house number, street name, locality, post office, and PIN code.
  4. Upload a high-resolution, legible scan or photo of your new residence address proof document. Keep file sizes under 2MB.
  5. Complete the simple text security captcha validation grid and click **Preview and Submit**.
Phase 3: Automated Cancellation & New Verification
  1. Save the unique **Reference ID string code** that appears on your confirmation window to track the status of your application.
  2. The system automatically alerts your old constituency desk to flag your entry for removal once the transfer is finalized.
  3. A local Booth Level Officer (BLO) tied to your *new* neighborhood will perform a quick field visit to confirm your residency proof.
  4. Once the review updates to approved, your old voter listing is purged, your profile updates, and a fresh physical smart card is mailed straight to your new doorstep via Speed Post.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • No Family Linkages Provided: When moving to a family member's household, failing to input the active Voter ID numbers of the family members already registered at that specific address can stall verification checks.
  • Uploading Unregistered Agreements: Uploading basic handwritten room receipts or unnotarized rental agreements that fail to meet legal verification standards as residential proofs.
  • Incomplete Boundary Parameters: Typing local street identifiers into the incorrect block boxes, which routes your application packet to the wrong assembly constituency office.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Are you unable to track your transfer application because your original transaction token receipt code was lost?
Do not submit a duplicate transfer request. Go to the NVSP homepage, open the **Search in Electoral Roll** utility tool, and type in your standard EPIC card index. The system will pull up your real-time entry status and show you whether your record is still processing or has successfully migrated to your new polling booth location.

FAQ

Q: Will my original Voter ID identification card number change after a transfer process is finalized?
A: No. Your core 10-character alphanumeric EPIC number is tied to you for life. Only your internal database mapping (such as your constituency name, assembly number, and polling booth station location) changes.


Q: Can I complete a voter transfer if I am relocating to another state for a temporary college program?
A: You can choose to transfer your registration if you consider your college address your ordinary place of residence. If you choose to do so, you must upload your official hostel allocation document or a student residency certificate as your primary proof of address.
