Safety Tips

🔒 Secure Data

Never share your online account passwords, banking PINs, or Aadhaar OTPs with anyone.

🌐 Check URLs

Always verify that the website URL addresses end strictly with .gov.in or .nic.in.

❌ Say No to Agents

Avoid unofficial third-party agents who charge huge margins for free public services.
