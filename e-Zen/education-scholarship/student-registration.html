What is this?

The Student Registration module is the foundational onboarding gate for all academic support systems, state concessions, and scholarship tracks. Completing this permanent profile creation issues your unique structural student tracking ID (such as the APAAR ID or NSP login key), linking your academic background with government data systems to unlock automated benefits.

Mandatory Prerequisite Rules

  • Aadhaar Data Harmony: The spelling of your name, gender code, and birth date must match exactly across your Aadhaar card and high school leaving marksheets.
  • Active Mobile Link: Your registered mobile phone number must be linked with your Aadhaar biometric profile to receive security OTP codes.
  • Single Profile Limitation: Creating multiple registration profiles using different mobile phone numbers or secondary identities will trigger an automated system ban.

Required Identity Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap targets to confirm document readiness states:

  • Aadhaar Card / E-Aadhaar PDF Print The primary identification anchor used for real-time biographical validation.
  • Matriculation (Class 10) Pass Certificate Used to verify school records, board roll numbers, and official birth year registries.
  • Active Private Mobile Connection Must remain active throughout your entire academic journey to receive processing notifications and renewal flags.
  • Valid E-Mail Profile Used to receive secure platform credentials, password recovery paths, and system confirmations.

Step-by-Step Registration Workflow

System Metric: 2026 Core Registry Protocol Security Validation: Direct UIDAI Gateway Sync
Phase 1: Identity Handshake
  1. Open the portal registration dashboard and select the "New Registration" option.
  2. Input your primary Aadhaar identification number and complete the mobile verification process using the security OTP code sent to your phone.
Phase 2: Password and Profile Configuration
  1. Upon confirmation of your biographical data from the database, the system generates your permanent Application ID.
  2. Create a secure custom password and configure your recovery email channels.
Phase 3: Base Academic Connection
  1. Log in using your newly generated credentials and complete the basic academic profile section.
  2. This profile acts as your master record, allowing you to easily apply for individual programs like Pre-Matric, Post-Matric, or E-Grantz schemes.

⚠️ Critical Obstacles & Validation Faults

  • Biographical Discrepancies: Minor typos or data mismatches (e.g., "Arun K." vs. "Arun Kumar") between school registries and Aadhaar profiles will cause an automated system rejection.
  • Aadhaar Mobile Unlinking: Being unable to complete registration due to your mobile number changing without updating your official biometric record.
  • Locked Identity Status: The registration fails because your Aadhaar profile biometric locking feature is active, preventing the portal from running validation queries.
