What is this?

An Equivalency Certificate is an official validation statement proving that a degree, diploma, or school program completed under an external board or foreign university is equal in value, structural content, and duration to the corresponding program offered by the native state board or university. It is required to qualify for local government employment exams and advanced academic enrollment rows.

Core Academic Benchmarks

  • The 70% Curricular Rule: To clear the review committee, the external degree's core syllabus blueprint must show at least a 70% alignment with the reference program's structure.
  • Structural Mode Parameter: Recognition maps strictly to regular programs. Distance education profiles must match specific University Grants Commission (UGC) or Association of Indian Universities (AIU) guidelines from high school (SSLC) onward.
  • Foreign Degree Tracking: Degrees from international campuses require a foundational evaluation certificate issued by the AIU before local university panels will review the folder.

Required Verification Folder (Checklist)

💡 Check off items to prepare your verification file:

  • Authenticated Scheme and Syllabus Blueprint Must be explicitly signed and stamped by the Registrar of the originating external university.
  • Consolidated Grade Sheet & Degree Certificate Clear digital copies of the original terminal degree and transcript records.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) or Mode Statement Official institutional proof verifying the regular or face-to-face nature of the course.
  • AIU Foreign Recognition Sheet (If Applicable) Required document if the degree was completed outside Indian academic lines.

Phase 1: Portal Profiling & Schema Setup
  1. Register on the university e-certificates engine using your primary mobile and email contact fields.
  2. Check the pre-approved database index to confirm if your specific course and board have already been granted equivalency rows.
Phase 2: Uploading Documents & Defect Audits
  1. Upload clean digital copies of your transcripts, transfer papers, and authorized syllabus documents.
  2. Monitor the portal dashboard for data queries. If the university flags a document defect, it must be resolved within 15 functional days.
Phase 3: Fee Payment & Certificate Download
  1. Once the Board of Studies verifies the file, complete the digital fee checkout (approximately ₹685 plus processing surcharges).
  2. Download your digitally generated, QR-mapped validation certificate directly from your account within 30 days of approval.

⚠️ Structural Barriers & Rejection Triggers

  • Nomenclature Mismatch: The application will fail if the degree title varies significantly, even if the course content overlaps with local programs.
  • Unauthenticated Syllabus Files: Submitting a standard, un-stamped internet copy of a syllabus instead of an official document signed by the external registrar.
  • Under-Age or Criteria Deficiencies: Failing to meet base requirements, such as minimum language counts or specific age cutoffs on the evaluation date.
