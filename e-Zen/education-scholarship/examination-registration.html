What is this?

The Examination Registration hub is the formal terminal window used to enroll in regular semester evaluations, annual board screenings, or specialized supplementary examination cycles. Completing this entry logs your specific elective course combinations, computes transaction fees based on practical lab tracks, and triggers the automated release of your center hall tickets.

Strict Institutional Guidelines

  • Attendance Threshold Constraint: Students must maintain a minimum cumulative attendance record (typically 75% or above) to clear automated portal blockades.
  • Elective Mapping Rules: Core courses, open electives, and language strings must match institutional records to prevent hall ticket configuration drops.
  • Condonation Protocol: Students with authorized medical leaves below the base attendance bar must upload medical board sheets and pay state condonation premiums prior to access closure.

Required Enrollment Ledger (Checklist)

💡 Check off items to verify your exam application package configuration:

  • Institutional Admission Roll / PRN Number Your permanent registration identity string used to map semester cycles.
  • Approved Course Elective Sheet Official course selection map verified by your department head or advisor.
  • Attendance Clearance Flag / Condonation Order Digital clearance string indicating compliance with the minimum institutional threshold.
  • Online Fee Transaction Receipt Digital receipt print showing complete clearance of theory, lab, and marksheet fees.

Filing & Admit Card Pipeline

System Framework: Active 2026 Examination Sync Timeline Rule: Late fee structures activate automatically post-deadline
Phase 1: Profile Course Verification
  1. Log into your institution's student examination console using your Permanent Register Number (PRN).
  2. Verify that the populated course codes match your current academic track, structural electives, and practical modules.
Phase 2: Fee Ledger Settlement
  1. Proceed to the online payment checkout interface to process itemized fee entries (theory charges, lab setups, and centralized camp costs).
  2. Ensure the transaction status updates to "Success" on your student ledger before closing the portal tab.
Phase 3: Hall Ticket Generation
  1. Following college approval of attendance records, your official Hall Ticket will populate inside the download console.
  2. Print the generated document, verify the designated examination center layout, and secure the signature of your head of institution.

⚠️ Common Registration Blocks & Rejections

  • Shortage of Attendance Blocks: The processing window remains bricked because attendance logs sit below institutional minimums without an approved condonation.
  • Unresolved Fee Discrepancies: The system holds your admit card because prior semester fee balances or supplementary desk fines remain unsettled.
  • Data Mismatches: Selecting incorrect subject codes during self-registration, creating a configuration error that blocks internal desk approval.
