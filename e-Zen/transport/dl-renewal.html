Driving Licence Renewal

Issuing Authority Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MoRTH) / RTO
Official Portal Sarathi Parivahan System
Prescribed Fee ₹200 statutory fee (+ ₹200 smart card fee if applicable)
Processing Time 15 to 30 Working Days
Open Sarathi Parivahan Portal ↗

What is this?

Driving Licence Renewal is the statutory compliance workflow required to extend the legal validity of a driver's credential under the Motor Vehicles Act. This process evaluates the licensee's ongoing physical fitness and updates biological indicators within the national Sarathi database register.

Why is it needed?

Operating a motor vehicle with an expired driving credential is a punishable offense that carries heavy penal fines. Maintaining an active renewal also protects the holder's vehicular insurance policy protections, which automatically become null and void if a claim is submitted while using an expired licence sheet.

Eligibility

  • Application Window: Drivers can apply for renewal up to one year before the expiry date, or within one year after the expiry date without needing a fresh driving re-test.
  • Age Rule: Applicants over 40 years of age, or holders of commercial transport vehicle badges, must submit an official medical assessment to validate their fitness.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what parameters you have prepared for upload:

  • Original Expired Driving Licence The original physical smart card or book licence to be processed for updating.
  • Form 1 & Form 1A Medical Certificate Form 1 self-declaration (for under 40). Form 1A must be signed and certified digitally by a registered government medical doctor (mandatory if over age 40).
  • Identity and Current Address Proof Clear photocopies of Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Voter ID to ensure regional jurisdiction parameters map correctly.

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: June 2026 Source: Parivahan MoRTH Desk
Before You Begin
  • ⚡ Check Contactless Eligibility: If your current driving licence is already linked to your verified Aadhaar card profile, you may qualify for "Fully Contactless Aadhaar Authentication" processing. This allows you to complete the entire renewal pipeline online without needing to physically visit an RTO counter.
Phase 1: Digital Application Initiation
  1. Click Open Sarathi Parivahan Portal above, select your respective State, and choose **Apply for DL Renewal**.
  2. Enter your exact Driving Licence alphanumeric string and your date of birth. Click **Get DL Details**.
  3. Verify that the generated name, photograph, and vehicle class categories match your existing credentials.
  4. Select the required transaction checkbox for **Renewal** and proceed to confirm your current state and RTO jurisdiction parameters.
Phase 2: Document Upload & Biometric Mapping
  1. Fill out the Form 1 physical self-declaration questionnaire on the screen.
  2. If you are over 40 years old, upload a clear scan of the signed **Form 1A Medical Certificate** obtained from a certified doctor.
  3. Upload scanned copies of your original driving licence along with current address proof documents.
  4. Upload a recent high-contrast passport photograph and a clear digital image of your signature matching the size specifications.
Phase 3: Fee Submission & Verification
  1. Navigate to the secure payment tab and complete the statutory renewal fee transaction via the integrated e-treasury gateway.
  2. Download and save the printed application reference receipt layout containing your unique application number string.
  3. For non-Aadhaar or commercial cases, book an appointment slot and visit the physical RTO with originals. For Aadhaar-verified contactless submissions, your application moves directly to the inspector dashboard for digital approval.
  4. Once verified, the updated licence is printed and dispatched to your registered home address via secure speed post delivery.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Mismatches in Biographical Names: If the name character layout on your older licence differs from your attached Aadhaar profile strings, the verification algorithm will flag it as a data conflict.
  • Invalid Form 1A Issuance: Uploading a medical certificate where the doctor's registration credential number is illegible, unverified, or missing an official counter-signature.
  • Unpaid Out-of-State Challans: Active, unresolved traffic enforcement penalties or pending challans flagged against your licence string will block the renewal pipeline until completely settled.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Has your driving licence been expired for more than one continuous year?
Do not try to submit a standard renewal application. Under updated statutory transport guidelines, if a licence remains expired for more than 12 months, you are legally required to undergo a physical driving re-test before the RTO. You must log in to the portal to file a renewal along with a re-test slot fee, book a physical test appointment, and complete the track evaluation to reactivate your driving privileges.

FAQ

Q: Can I apply for a licence renewal at my current workplace city RTO if my original licence card was issued in another state?
A: Yes. You can apply at your current residential location RTO. The Sarathi portal will automatically initiate an online Address Change transaction alongside the renewal request, pulling your historical data index across state lines securely.


Q: Am I legally permitted to drive on public roads using the printed application payment receipt while my physical smart card is being printed?
A: Yes. The printed application summary accompanied by the verified payment receipt acts as a temporary legal authorization ledger for up to 30 days or until the final smart card layout is processed and delivered.
