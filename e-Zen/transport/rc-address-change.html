What is this?

A Vehicle RC Address Change is an administrative database update designed to reflect a vehicle owner's new residential location on their vehicle registration card. Regulated under Section 49 of the Motor Vehicles Act, this update keeps your vehicle profile compliant within the national registry after a move.

Why is it needed?

By law, vehicle owners must report a change of address within 30 days of relocating. Keeping your address updated ensures that tax communications, traffic enforcement alerts, and physical notices are sent to the correct location. It also helps avoid claim delays with insurance providers, who require matching address details to process claims smoothly.

Eligibility

  • Timing: The update request should be submitted within 30 days of establishing your new residence.
  • Clearance Status: The vehicle file must be free of active judicial holds, vehicle blacklistings, or unresolved traffic challans.

Required Documents (Checklist)

💡 Tap the boxes to track what parameters you have structured for upload:

  • Form 33 The official standard statutory notice layout used to report an alteration of residence details.
  • Original Physical Registration Certificate (RC) Your existing physical smart card or paper registry sheet, which will be updated in the system.
  • Valid New Address Proof An updated Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Voter ID that clearly displays your new residential address.
  • Unexpired Insurance & PUCC sheets Valid emission compliance sheets and vehicle insurance policies to confirm operational status.

Step-by-Step Procedure

Last Verified: June 2026 Source: Ministry of Road Transport & Highways Dashboard
Before You Begin
  • 💡 Inter-State Relocations Require an NOC: If you are relocating your vehicle across state lines, you must secure an official No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your previous home RTO before updating your address.
Phase 1: Verification and Transaction Choice
  1. Click **Open Vahan Parivahan Portal** above, enter your vehicle registration number string, and confirm.
  2. Provide the final 5 digits of your physical chassis number string to unlock your records.
  3. Navigate to the services dashboard panel and select **Change of Address**.
  4. Fill out the data forms with your new residential information: State, District, and specific RTO zone.
Phase 2: Document Attestation Upload
  1. Download the system-compiled layout copy of **Form 33** generated by your data entry.
  2. Sign the application sheet and upload it back to the portal interface.
  3. Upload clean digital copies of your original registration card, new address proofs, active insurance, and valid emissions sheets.
Phase 3: Fee Submission and Production
  1. Complete the smart card reissue processing fee payment via the integrated online treasury portal.
  2. Print the payment confirmation receipt and the completed Form 33 application summary.
  3. Aadhaar-verified contactless applications are routed directly to the system inspector dashboard. For traditional tracking methods, drop off your physical application folder at the RTO window.
  4. The updated vehicle registration card is printed and delivered directly to your new address via post.

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

  • Address Discrepancies: Small spelling variations or mismatched PIN codes between your online data entry and your uploaded identity documents.
  • Missing Inter-State Tax Settlements: Relocating across state lines without settling regional road tax differentials or attaching a valid RTO clearance certificate.
  • Expired Vehicle Fitness Certificates: Attempting to change location information for a commercial or private vehicle whose core fitness timeline has expired.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Are you moving across state lines and worried about paying double road tax on your vehicle?
By law, you must pay the road tax for your new state when processing an inter-state address update. However, you can claim a refund from your previous state. Once your new address change is registered and active, submit a formal tax refund application to your original RTO along with a copy of your new registration card to claim your prorated tax balance back.

FAQ

Q: Am I required to change my physical vehicle license plate when updating my address to a new state?
A: Yes. For inter-state relocations, you will be assigned a new state registration code string. You must update your physical license plate to match the new number within the designated timeframe.


Q: Can a company-owned corporate vehicle process an address change online using an Aadhaar profile?
A: No. Corporate-owned vehicle profiles do not qualify for contactless personal Aadhaar authentication. Company vehicle transfers must be submitted with official corporate board resolutions and authorized signatures at the physical RTO window.
